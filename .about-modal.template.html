RioRacer: Dog vs. Street

Free Endless Runner Game

Welcome to RioRacer, the most intense browser-based endless runner game! Take control of Rio, a brave dog navigating the chaotic streets filled with obstacles. Test your reflexes, master the double-jump mechanic, and compete on the global leaderboard.

How to Play

  • Jump: Press SPACEBAR or TAP the screen to jump over obstacles
  • Double Jump: Press SPACEBAR again while in the air for extra height
  • Golden Bone: Collect power-ups to transform into Super Rio and smash through enemies!
  • Survive: Dodge stray cats and dogs. One hit and it's game over!
